About Me

Hey there! I see you have landed on my about me page. What is there to know one might say? Well, let’s get a little acquainted.

My name is Michael and I’m a graduate of SUNY Cortland with a Bachelor degree in Communications. Yes, a Communications Major….I know. After graduating I was able to obtain a job in the finance industry through Wells Fargo. From there I have built relationships with my clients and developed professional strengths. Though I had a stellar reputation with the company, I found myself wanting a change. I decided to explore and challenge myself in a new profession, specifically in the tech industry. That is when I came across programming. I knew it was a perfect fit. It challenged me and allowed me to use my creative side to solve problems.

To gain more of a formal education in programming, I enrolled and completed a web development program through UNCC with the purpose of becoming a full stack developer. This new drive and passion has allowed me to receive the challenge of non-stop learning and use of my creative side. I am now able to develop applications on both front and back-end technologies. As you browse through my site you will see various applications I’ve created showcasing my skillset. I will continue to update and develop new applications to post on the site so make sure to check back often. If you have questions please contact me on LinkedIn or within the contact section on my site!
